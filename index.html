About Us

Formed in December of 2021, this alternative / pop punk band from the south of Metro Manila was initially a home recording project by JM, Wency, Arbie, and Perry. The group DIY'ed everything from recording all the way to publishing and marketing. They released two singles, "Where Flowers Used to Bloom" and "Orora" in 2022. Unforeseen challenges arose, including the extended global pandemic restrictions, multiple lineup changes, and schedule and financial constraints. These obstacles led to a premature one-year hiatus beginning in mid-2022.

Refueled by their passion for songwriting and recording, they regrouped in June of 2023, and wrote one song per week for three months. Eyeing to take their talents from their bedrooms to the live gigging scene, they recruited John and Lyrics to complete the full-band lineup. They recorded and released their single "Wander Off" and DIY'ed its music video production. They were then contacted and invited to perform their first live gig on the global phenomenon Wish Bus.

Now signed under KDR Music House , Mundane is geared up and ready to bring its DIY ethos, hybrid sound, and heartfelt lyrics to the forefront. Their latest single, a remake of their very first song, "Where Flowers Used to Bloom" is about the members' shared experience on liminality and the loss of one's own passion and identity, and it was re-released under KDR Music House on June 28, 2024.

Currently, the 5 members are writing non-stop and recording their next single with an eye (and ear) for a continuous organic evolution of their sound.

Stay tuned!
